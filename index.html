I. Reference Tool Only. CARL is an automated reference tool that retrieves and evaluates regulatory citations. It does not replace professional engineering judgment, code authority interpretation, or official inspection findings.
II. Citation Verification Required. All citations, regulatory references, and compliance evaluations generated by CARL must be independently verified against current editions of the applicable standards before use in official documentation.

IV. Data Handling. Observations, photos, and evaluation results are transmitted to cloud-based processing services. Do not submit classified, controlled unclassified information, or personally identifiable information through this tool.
V. No Warranty. CARL is provided as-is with no warranty of accuracy, completeness, or fitness for any particular purpose. Citation accuracy depends on the quality and currency of the reference library.
